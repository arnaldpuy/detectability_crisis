\subsection{Does harmonization change the disagreement

\begin{Shaded}
\begin{Highlighting}[]
\CommentTok{\# DOES HARMONIZATION CHANGE THE DISAGREEMENT CURVES? \#\#\#\#\#\#\#\#\#\#\#\#\#\#\#\#\#\#\#\#\#\#\#\#\#\#\#}
\CommentTok{\#}
\CommentTok{\# The main results of the paper are disagreement curves: the fraction of grid}
\CommentTok{\# cells that disagree on irrigation presence as a function of the detectability}
\CommentTok{\# threshold tau, together with their decomposition into existential vs marginal,}
\CommentTok{\# minor vs major disagreement, etc.}
\CommentTok{\#}
\CommentTok{\# Even if we have shown that:}
\CommentTok{\#   {-} global irrigated{-}area totals are conserved, and}
\CommentTok{\#   {-} irrigation prevalence is stable under nearest{-}neighbour reprojection,}
\CommentTok{\# this does not automatically guarantee that the shape of the disagreement}
\CommentTok{\# curves is unaffected by harmonization.}
\CommentTok{\#}
\CommentTok{\# In this QA we therefore explicitly test whether the harmonization step}
\CommentTok{\# (reprojection + resampling) biases disagreement metrics across tau by:}
\CommentTok{\#   1) computing disagreement curves on:}
\CommentTok{\#        (a) the baseline aligned 0.2° stack (mha\_stack\_ll\_02),}
\CommentTok{\#       (b) the nearest{-}neighbour round{-}trip, renormalized (mha\_ll02\_target\_near\_cons),}
\CommentTok{\#      (c) the bilinear round{-}trip, renormalized (mha\_ll02\_target\_bilin\_cons);}
\CommentTok{\#   2) comparing curves (b) and (c) against the baseline across the full tau grid;}
\CommentTok{\#   3) summarizing for each key metric the maximum absolute change across tau.}
\CommentTok{\#}
\CommentTok{\# If near\_minus\_base deltas are c. 0 for all tau, harmonization does not distort}
\CommentTok{\# disagreement results. If bilin\_minus\_base deltas are large at low tau, this}
\CommentTok{\# indicates that bilinear smoothing inflates the spatial footprint of small}
\CommentTok{\# irrigation signals and artificially increases disagreement near the detection}
\CommentTok{\# limit.}
